# TrackHire

TrackHire is a web application designed to centralize and simplify the job search process. Instead of juggling information across multiple platforms like LinkedIn, Indeed, and Handshake, this tool organizes your applications, interviews, and deadlines into one streamlined system. It’s built specifically for students and professionals who want a more structured and less overwhelming way to manage their career journey.

## Key Features
* **Centralized Dashboard**: View and manage all your job application data, including titles, companies, and statuses, in one place.
* **Visual Status Tracking**: Use color-coded badges (e.g., green for offers, red for rejections) to see your progress at a glance.
* **Interview Management**: Keep track of upcoming interview schedules and technical assessments so nothing falls through the cracks.
* **Filtering & Sorting**: Easily organize your applications by status or category to focus on your most active leads.
* **Data Security**: Secure account management featuring hashed passwords and protected database queries to keep your personal records safe.

## Tech Stack
* **Languages**: Java, JavaScript, and SQL
* **Database**: MySQL
* **Server**: Apache Tomcat
